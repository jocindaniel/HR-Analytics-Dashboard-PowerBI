**Project Overview**
This HR Analytics Dashboard provides insights into employee attrition, headcount, and salary trends. The dashboard helps HR teams monitor workforce metrics and identify areas requiring attention.

**Business Problem**
Employee attrition impacts organizational productivity and increases recruitment costs. This dashboard enables HR teams to analyze attrition patterns and make informed workforce decisions.

**Tools Used**
Power BI
Power Query
DAX
Excel/CSV Dataset
Data Visualization Techniques

**Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**
Total Headcount: 15
Total Attrition: 4
Attrition Rate: 26.67%
Average Monthly Income: ₹5.52K

**Dashboard Features**
Department-wise attrition analysis
Average monthly income analysis by job role
Workforce KPI monitoring
Interactive visualizations for employee insights

**Key Insights**
Overall attrition rate stands at 26.67%.
Attrition is concentrated in specific departments.
Salary distribution varies significantly across job roles.
The dashboard helps HR teams identify trends and improve employee retention strategies.
